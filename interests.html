Fitness & Movement

Outside of school and work, I enjoy exploring hobbies that challenge me both physically and creatively. I love finding new ways to push myself through different forms of movement and fitness. Some of my favorite activities include weightlifting, long-distance running, Pilates, and yoga, and recently I've been experimenting with learning calisthenics as well. I enjoy the process of setting new physical goals and seeing how consistency and discipline translate into progress over time.

Pottery & Craftsmanship

I'm also really interested in pottery and hope to spend more time developing that skill. One of my goals is to eventually create a full handmade dinnerware set — including plates, bowls, and mugs — that I can use when I move into my own place after college. I love how pottery combines creativity with patience and craftsmanship.

Travel & Exploration

Travel is another big interest of mine, and I've been fortunate to visit over 15 countries. I enjoy experiencing new cultures, environments, and perspectives. One of my most memorable recent trips was to Hawaii with friends, where we explored Hawai'i Volcanoes National Park, swam with manta rays at night, and spent time relaxing by the beach. Traveling continues to be one of my favorite ways to learn about the world and step outside my everyday routine.
