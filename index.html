About Me

Hi, I'm Maya! I'm a Computer Science and Marketing Analytics student at Northeastern University with a passion for using technology and data to solve meaningful business problems. I'm particularly interested in how data-driven insights can improve decision-making, streamline processes, and create more impactful products and services.

Through Northeastern's co-op program, I've gained experience in technology risk and IT auditing. I worked as an IT Auditor at TJX Companies and as a Technology Risk Consulting co-op at PwC, where I helped evaluate technology controls, assess risks, and support organizations in strengthening their systems and processes.

Outside of academics and work, I enjoy staying active and creative. In my free time, you can usually find me running, working out, baking something new, or spending time in a pottery studio. I also love to travel and have visited 15+ countries, which has helped shape my curiosity about different cultures and perspectives. A fun fact about me is that I lived in South India from ages 6–11. I also enjoy spending time with friends and family, whether that's trying new restaurants, exploring the city, or just relaxing together.
